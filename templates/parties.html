Parties

Sharp Sparks provides entertainment for children’s birthday parties.

We love to entertain children at birthday parties. We offer a range of activities, which can be theme to meet your needs.

  • Etextiles – electronics ( small amount of sewing required)
  • Computer programming
  • Robotics
  • Science Demonstrations
  • Craft Activities

The space requirements depend on the type of party required. For computer programming access to multiple power plugs is a requirement. For science- based parties’ table space with floor room for the children to sit is all that is needed.
